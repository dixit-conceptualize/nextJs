AD Food Hub’s Economic contribution

Abu Dhabi Food Hub is designed to support hundreds of tenants and contribute significantly to the UAE’s vision for economic diversification and food trade growth.

Abu Dhabi Food Hub is a key economic enabler driving the UAE’s non-oil growth and regional food trade leadership. As a public-private partnership between AD Ports Group and Ghassan Aboud Group, the Hub strengthens Abu Dhabi’s position as a gateway to the GCC, Middle East, Africa, and Asia.

Strategically located within KEZAD and connected through world-class multimodal transport, it facilitates seamless cross-border trade, job creation, and SME activation. By attracting foreign investment and empowering regional producers, the Hub contributes to a more resilient, diversified economy — aligning with the UAE’s vision for sustainable growth and long-term food security.
