At the heart of Abu Dhabi Food Hub lies a series of purpose-built Markets and Pavilions — a dynamic ecosystem that brings together every segment of the global food value chain under one roof.

Each pavilion is designed around a specific product category, from fresh produce and dry commodities to seafood, dairy, and gourmet foods, ensuring operational efficiency and optimal handling standards. Strategically located within KEZAD, the Markets serve as a central meeting point for producers, distributors, and buyers from across the world.

Supported by world-class logistics, integrated cold chains, and digital trade systems, they simplify the movement of goods from source to shelf. More than a place to trade, the Markets represent a new model of collaboration and efficiency — connecting businesses, cultures, and opportunities to shape the future of food commerce in the region and beyond.

Food Trade Zones & Spaces

Strategically planned,
with a space for everyone.

Beyond its thriving markets, Abu Dhabi Food Hub offers a complete ecosystem of spaces designed to power every layer of the food value chain. From behind-the-scenes logistics to customer-facing experiences, these zones create an environment where business efficiency meets innovation. Flexible warehouses, cold chain facilities, and complementary activity zones enable seamless operations and value-added services, while the Farmers Market, Food Studio, and Retail & F&B clusters foster connection, creativity, and community.

Supported by modern offices and integrated sustainability systems, these spaces are purpose-built to help businesses scale smarter, collaborate better, and thrive within the region’s most advanced food trade destination.
