Abu Dhabi Food Hub

Every detail of the Fruits & Vegetables Market is engineered for performance. With dedicated segments for Fruits & Vegetables, O&P, and a Crossdock facility for direct dispatch, the market ensures fast turnover of perishable goods while maintaining optimal quality. Its proximity to cold chain facilities, pre-cooling units, and customs checkpoints keeps produce fresh and market-ready — minimizing handling times and reducing spoilage.

KEZAD is participating in Future Food Forum.

For traders and distributors, the Hub’s integrated logistics ecosystem means less downtime and more opportunity. Whether you’re moving containers through Khalifa Port, storing in temperature-controlled warehouses, or distributing across the UAE, every link of the supply chain operates within reach. The on-site value-added services — from sorting and packaging to quality testing and labeling — further enhance efficiency and profitability.
